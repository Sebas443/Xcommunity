X-Men

They were born mutants...

posessing powers of a genetic

origin that made them outcasts

of society. But one man-Professor

Charles Xavier-brought them together

to learn to use their unique gifts

in the service of a world that hates

and fears them...They are the Children

of the Atom...

New Mutants

Born with an X-gene that grants

weird and uncanny abilities,

mutants are thought to be the

next step in human evolution.

Xi'an Coy Manh, a disciple of

the mutant activist Charles Xavier,

has assembled a team of uniquely

qualified mutants to investigate

phenomena of the weird and uncanny...

X-Factor

Originally a team comprising the

original five X-men, X-Factor

was eventually commanded by Havok

as a government task force. Jamie

Madrox later took the name to found

X-Factor Investigations and ultimately

sold it to Serval Industries, who

now run a corporate sponsored mutant

team, led by Polaris.

Info

Current Leader: Polaris

Current Roster: Polaris, Gambit

Quicksilver, Danger, Cypher, Warlock

First Appeared: X-Factor #1(1986)

X-Force

When Cable reformed the New Mutants,

he made them his soldiers. They

became a mutant 'black ops' team,

assigned to kill not just the enemies

of the X-Men, but of all mutantkind.

Although this team eventually dispersed,

the X-Force moniker has persisted in

successive iterations of more forceful

X-teams.

Info

Current Leader: Domino

Current Roster: Domino, Warpath

Cannonball, Shatterstar, Deathlok

Boom-Boom, Cable

First Appeared: The New Mutants #100(1991)

Avengers Unity Squad

with a goal to show the world

that mutants and humans can

work together.

Marvel Girl

Jean Grey was one of the

five original X-Men. An

omega-level mutant telekinetic

and telepath, Jean has gained

near limitless powers as a

recurrent host of the Phoenix

Force. She is known for her

many returns from death and

as the deceased wife of Cyclops.

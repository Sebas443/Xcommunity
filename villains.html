Brotherhood of Mutants

The Brotherhood of Mutants is a

group dedicated to the cause of

mutant superiority over humans.

Throughout its various incarnations,

the Brotherhood's agenda has gravitated

towards either subjugating the

human race to the will of mutants

or eradicating humanity altogether.
